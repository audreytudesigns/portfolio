28th Ave.

28th Ave. by Junha Park is an album that explores the adventure of navigating through reaching for your dreams, regret, and struggles in your twenties, which he reflects at 27. Stars of all shapes, sizes, and physical conditions cut out of music sheets, representing the struggles of reaching for your stars.
