DID YOU KNOW?

OVERVIEW

Did You Know? is a campaign that aims to inspire curiosity and compassion for endangered elephants among children while inviting people of all ages to engage and learn. Elephants are a keystone species that plays a pivotal role in shaping our world, but there is not much attention that is brought to this issue. Through a friendly, fun, and whimsical approach, it highlights why elephants are vital to our world and why protecting them matters.
