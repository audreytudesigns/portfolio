TRUNKS & LEAVES

Trunks & Leaves is a non-profit organization based in Sri Lanka dedicated to the preservation and peaceful coexistence of people and elephants. Their mission is to raise awareness about Asian elephants and their habitats, locally and globally, compiling resources and tools for education, research and conservation. This logo redesign project aimed to rethink and redesign the identity to simplify and establish what Trunks & Leaves is about. The final logo combines an elephant and leaf to capture the warm, friendly, and playful energy of this organization.
