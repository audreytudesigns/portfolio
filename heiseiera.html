Heisei: The Era

Heisei: The Era is a deep dive web design project on Japan’s Heisei Era, which lasted from 1989-2019. This site explores 5 different categories (Historical events, technological advancements, fashion, music, and the economy) that defined one of Japan’s most iconic eras.
This website, inspired by the quirky and fun aesthetics of early technology and Japanese trends, allows users to learn more about this era of Japan, which HYPER! is inspired by, in a fun and interactive way!
