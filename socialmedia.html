Communication Design Social Media

As the Social Media Manager of the Communication Design Program’s social media account, my job is documenting all the exciting things that happen in the program and keeping our students updated. Connecting with professors, students, and the Communication Design Program Chair, I am part of planning, documenting, and promoting events/activities. Some of my recent projects are informational graphics for the Design Lecture Series, a teaser campaign for the Communication Design Spring Blowout, and photographing the Book Share event that I planned alongside my CD Club Co-president.
