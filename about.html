Hi! I’m Audrey Tu! I am a multidisciplinary designer with a love for stories and adventure. I love to explore. Whether it is the world itself, through stories and experiences, or as I travel to new places, I continue to broaden my perspective and inspirations, which I bring to everything that I do and design. I specialize in brand identities and visual systems and have a growing experience in motion and web design. In my work, I prioritize creating things that are both fun and functional, continuously drawing inspiration from my adventures and the world around me.
